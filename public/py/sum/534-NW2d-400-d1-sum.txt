The majority quotes with approval the general rule that whether money found is treasure trove, mislaid, abandoned, or lost property is a fact question.
After finding the money, Benjamin proceeded to give written notice of finding the property as prescribed in Iowa Code chapter 644 (1993), "Lost Property."
Benjamin followed the law in giving legal notice of finding property.
No claim was made by the owner after legally prescribed notice was given that it had been found.
 I would hold that Benjamin is legally entitled to the entire amount of money that he found in the airplane wing as the owner of abandoned property
