Does a landlord's covenant to refund a tenant's security deposit run with the land, thus obligating a successor landlord to refund it?
Thereafter, it brought this action against Growth. The trial court held that the covenant to refund the security deposit ran with the land and bound Growth through the indemnification clause in the sales contract between Growth and the City. Growth appeals, contending that the covenant did not run.
The City had no obligation to return the security deposit.
We need not consider further arguments concerning the effect of the transactions between the City and Growth or the City and Mullendore. Reversed
